<p><ul><li>Full-Custom Design</li><li>Standard-Cell Based</li><li>Gate-Array Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) are customized chips designed for specific applications. The market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Full-Custom Design**: Offers maximum performance and optimization, tailored for unique functions but requires extensive design time and cost.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Standard-Cell Based**: Utilizes pre-designed cells for faster development, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness while still allowing customization.</p><p>3. **Gate-Array Based**: Provides flexibility with a pre-defined array of gates, enabling quicker time-to-market with moderate customization potential but less optimal performance compared to full-custom designs.</p></p>
