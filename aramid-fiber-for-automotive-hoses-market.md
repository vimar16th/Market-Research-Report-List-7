<p><ul><li>Para Aramid Fiber</li><li>Meta Aramid Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aramid fibers are high-performance synthetic fibers used in automotive hoses due to their exceptional strength, heat resistance, and durability. Para aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are known for their tensile strength and resistance to abrasion, making them ideal for high-stress applications like brake lines and fuel hoses. In contrast, meta aramid fibers, such as Nomex, offer superior thermal stability and are often used in applications requiring heat resistance, such as engine hoses and protective gear. Both types enhance performance and longevity in automotive applications.</p></p>
