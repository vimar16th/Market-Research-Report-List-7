<p><p>The area scan sensor market is primarily categorized into three types: Mono, GBR, and NIR sensors. Mono sensors capture grayscale images, offering high resolution and contrast for applications where color is not a priority. GBR sensors utilize red, green, and blue filters to produce full-color imagery, ideal for detailed visual inspections. NIR sensors detect near-infrared light, enabling enhanced imaging in low-light conditions or for specific materials, facilitating applications in agriculture, medical imaging, and more. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
