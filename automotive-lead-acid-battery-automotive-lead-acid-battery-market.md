<p><ul><li>VRLA Battery</li><li>Flooded Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive lead-acid battery market primarily includes two types: VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) and Flooded Batteries. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and use a valve to regulate gas release, making them safer and suitable for modern vehicles. Flooded batteries, also known as wet-cell batteries, require regular maintenance, including water refilling, and are commonly used in traditional automotive applications. Both types provide reliable power for starting, lighting, and ignition systems, with VRLA batteries gaining popularity due to their convenience and safety features.</p></p>
