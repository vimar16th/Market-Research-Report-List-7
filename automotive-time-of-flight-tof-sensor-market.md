<p><strong>The Automotive Time of Flight ToF Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ToF Sensors</li><li>Indirect ToF Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Time of Flight (ToF) Sensor market is primarily divided into two types: Direct ToF Sensors and Indirect ToF Sensors. Direct ToF Sensors measure the time it takes for light to travel directly from the source to the object and back, providing accurate distance measurements in real-time. In contrast, Indirect ToF Sensors estimate distance by analyzing the phase shift of modulated light waves reflected off an object. Both types play critical roles in advanced driver-assistance systems and autonomous vehicle technologies.</p></p>
