<p><strong>The Bicycle Front Fork Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coil</li><li>Resistance Glue</li><li>Oil-Coil</li><li>Oil-Air</li><li>Air-Air</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bicycle front fork market offers various types of fork suspension systems, each suited for different riding styles. Coil forks use a metal coil spring for support, providing a comfortable ride. Resistance glue forks employ a viscous fluid for damping. Oil-coil systems combine oil for compression damping with a coil spring. Oil-air forks utilize air pressure with oil for adjustability and responsiveness. Air-air forks depend solely on air pressure for suspension, offering lightweight and customizable performance, ideal for advanced riders.</p></p>
