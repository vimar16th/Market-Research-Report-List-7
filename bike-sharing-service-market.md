<p><p>Bike-sharing services are primarily categorized into dockless and station-based markets. In dockless systems, bikes can be unlocked and parked anywhere within a designated area, offering flexibility and convenience. Users locate and rent bikes via mobile apps, promoting spontaneous use. Conversely, station-based systems require users to pick up and return bikes at fixed stations. This model encourages more organized usage and predictable locations, making it easier for operators to manage and maintain their fleets while ensuring availability at designated hubs.</p></p>
