<p><p>The Biopharma Track and Trace Solutions market is divided into two primary types: barcodes and RFID. Barcodes involve the use of printed labels containing unique identifiers that can be scanned, facilitating streamlined tracking of pharmaceutical products throughout the supply chain. RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) employs radio waves to automatically identify and track tags attached to products, offering enhanced real-time visibility and data collection. Both technologies improve inventory management, prevent counterfeiting, and ensure compliance with regulatory standards in biopharma distribution.</p></p>
