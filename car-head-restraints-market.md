<p><p>The car head restraints market is divided into two primary types: fixed and adjustable car head restraints. Fixed car head restraints are permanently positioned and offer a basic level of safety by limiting head movement during a collision. In contrast, adjustable car head restraints can be repositioned for better comfort and support, enhancing protection for a wider range of occupants. Both types aim to reduce the risk of whiplash injuries, but they cater to different consumer preferences and safety requirements.</p></p>
