<p><p>The centrifugal clutches market can be categorized into three main types: mechanical, spring, and others. Mechanical centrifugal clutches rely on inertia to engage and disengage, often used in high-performance applications. Spring-based centrifugal clutches utilize springs to control engagement speed and torque transfer, providing smoother operation in various vehicles. Other types encompass specialized designs, like hydraulic or electromagnetic clutches, catering to specific industries or unique performance requirements. Each type serves distinct functions and applications across automotive, industrial, and recreational sectors.</p></p>
