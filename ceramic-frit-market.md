<p><p>The ceramic frit market is primarily divided into two categories: leaded frit and lead-free frit. Leaded frit contains lead compounds, which enhance glossiness and thermal stability but raise health concerns due to lead toxicity. Conversely, lead-free frit is formulated using alternative materials to eliminate lead, making it safer for both consumers and the environment while still providing desirable aesthetic and functional properties. The shift towards lead-free options is driven by stricter regulations and growing consumer awareness of health risks.</p></p>
