<p><p>The chemical processing catalyst market can be categorized into two main types: active catalysts and deactivation catalysts. Active catalysts facilitate chemical reactions, enhancing efficiency and selectivity in processes like petrochemical production and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, deactivation catalysts focus on managing or mitigating the effects of catalyst deterioration over time, ensuring sustained performance and longevity. Both types are crucial for optimizing production processes, improving yield, and reducing the costs and environmental impact associated with chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
