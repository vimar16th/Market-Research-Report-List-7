<p><strong>The CMOS Sensors for Industrial Vision Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Shutter</li><li>Global Shutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CMOS sensors for industrial vision are essential for capturing high-quality images in various applications. The two primary types are rolling shutter and global shutter. Rolling shutter captures images line by line, making it suitable for static scenes but prone to distortion in fast-moving subjects. In contrast, global shutter captures the entire frame simultaneously, eliminating motion artifacts, making it ideal for dynamic environments. Each type addresses specific needs in the industrial sector, influencing performance, speed, and application suitability.</p></p>
