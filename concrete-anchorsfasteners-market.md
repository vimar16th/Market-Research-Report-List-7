<p><strong>The Concrete Anchors/Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Anchors/Fasteners</li><li>Adhesive Anchors/Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete anchors/fasteners market is categorized into two primary types: mechanical anchors and adhesive anchors. Mechanical anchors rely on physical engagement and friction with the concrete to provide strong hold, making them ideal for structural applications. Common types include expansion bolts and wedge anchors. Adhesive anchors, on the other hand, use epoxy or other adhesives to bond the anchor to the concrete, offering high load-bearing capacity and flexibility in installation, especially in hollow or cracked concrete situations.</p></p>
