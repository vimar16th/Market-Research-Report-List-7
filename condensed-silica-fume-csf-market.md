The market is segmented into three types: Densified Silica Fume, which has a higher bulk density and improved flowability, making it suitable for large-scale concrete applications; Semi Densified Silica Fume, which offers a balance between handling and performance; and Undensified Silica Fume, characterized by a lower density, typically used in specialized applications needing finer particles. Each type varies in performance and usage depending on project requirements.</p></p>
