<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Copper Foil for Printed Circuit Board (PCB) market is primarily divided into two types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is manufactured by mechanically rolling copper into thin sheets, offering excellent flexibility and uniform thickness, ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced through an electroplating process, allowing for finer details and intricate patterns. This type is commonly used in high-capacity and high-performance electronic devices, enhancing conductivity and reliability in PCBs.</p></p>
