<p><p>CPU Heat Sink Paste, also known as thermal paste or thermal compound, is a thermally conductive material applied between a CPU (Central Processing Unit) and its heat sink. The rise of overclocking practices among gaming enthusiasts and the increasing demand for energy-efficient cooling solutions are further propelling market growth.
