<p><p>The custom dentures market encompasses two primary types: removable and fixed dentures. Removable dentures are designed to be taken out for cleaning and maintenance, offering flexibility and ease of use for patients. In contrast, fixed dentures are securely attached to dental implants or existing teeth, providing a more permanent solution and greater stability, which closely mimics the feel and function of natural teeth. Both options cater to varying patient needs and preferences, enhancing dental aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
