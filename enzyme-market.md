<p><strong>The Enzyme Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidoreductases</li><li>Transferases</li><li>Hydrolases</li><li>Isomerases</li><li>Lyases</li><li>Ligases</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The enzyme market comprises several key types based on their functions: </p><p>1. **Oxidoreductases** facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, impacting metabolic processes. </p><p>2. **Transferases** transfer functional groups between molecules, crucial in biosynthesis. </p><p>3. **Hydrolases** catalyze hydrolysis reactions, aiding in digestion and polymer breakdown. </p><p>4. **Isomerases** convert molecules into their isomers, essential for metabolic flexibility. </p><p>5. **Lyases** break chemical bonds by means other than hydrolysis or oxidation, playing roles in various metabolic pathways. </p><p>6. **Ligases** join two molecules together, often requiring ATP, vital for DNA and protein synthesis.</p></p>
