<p><p>The enzyme preparations market is categorized into six main types based on their catalytic functions. Oxidoreductases facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, while transferases move functional groups between molecules. Hydrolases catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking down compounds with water. Isomerases alter molecule structures without changing their composition, and lyases cleave bonds by means other than hydrolysis or oxidation. Lastly, ligases join two molecules together, often requiring ATP. Together, these enzyme types play crucial roles in various industries, including biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
