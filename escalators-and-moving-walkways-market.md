<p><p>The escalators and moving walkways market can be categorized into two primary types: parallel and multi-parallel systems. Parallel systems consist of individual units operating side by side, often installed in high-traffic areas like airports or shopping malls to efficiently transport people between different levels or sections. In contrast, multi-parallel systems feature several escalators or walkways aligned in succession, enhancing capacity and flow, making them ideal for larger venues or spaces where increased passenger movement is required.</p></p>
