<p><p>Explosion relief doors are critical safety devices designed to prevent the catastrophic buildup of pressure during an explosion. Bottom hinged explosion relief doors open outward from the bottom, providing a quick release of pressure while requiring minimal ceiling space. Conversely, top hinged explosion relief doors pivot from the top, allowing for efficient pressure relief in confined spaces and are often used where vertical clearance is limited. Both types are essential in industries handling flammable materials to ensure safe operation and compliance.</p></p>
