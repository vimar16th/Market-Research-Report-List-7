In contrast, integral farm duty motors are higher-powered, delivering more substantial torque and performance for demanding operations, such as large irrigation systems and heavy farm machinery. Both types enable efficient agricultural processes, optimizing energy use while meeting diverse operational requirements in farming environments.</p></p>
