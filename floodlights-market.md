<p><p>Floodlights can be categorized into two primary market types: metal-halide lamp and LED lamp. Metal-halide floodlights are traditionally used for outdoor lighting due to their high intensity and broad spectrum of light, making them suitable for large areas. However, they consume more energy and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, LED floodlights offer energy efficiency, longer life, and lower heat output, making them increasingly popular for both residential and commercial applications. The shift towards sustainability drives the growth of the LED segment.</p></p>
