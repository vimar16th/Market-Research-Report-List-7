<p><p>The Fourier-transform Infrared Spectroscopy (FTIR) fuel analyzer market primarily features two types of beam splitters: ZnSe-based and KBr-based. ZnSe-based beam splitters are favored for their broad spectral range and higher sensitivity in mid-infrared applications, making them suitable for analyzing various fuel types. KBr-based beam splitters, while offering good performance, are more temperature sensitive and less robust. Both types enable accurate characterization of fuel properties through infrared absorption spectra, essential for quality control and regulatory compliance in the fuel industry.</p></p>
