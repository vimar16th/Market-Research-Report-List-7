<p><p>The grid-connected microgrid market comprises three main types: AC microgrids, DC microgrids, and hybrid microgrids. AC microgrids utilize alternating current for compatibility with the existing grid, facilitating energy exchange. DC microgrids focus on direct current, making them efficient for renewable sources like solar and battery systems. Hybrid microgrids combine both AC and DC systems, optimizing energy use and flexibility. Each type aims to enhance grid reliability, integrate renewable energy, and improve local energy management.</p></p>
