<p><strong>The Harrows Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disk Harrow</li><li>Tine Harrow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The harrow market is classified into two main types: disk harrows and tine harrows. Disk harrows utilize flat, round blades to break up soil and efficiently mix organic matter, making them ideal for heavier, compacted soils. Tine harrows, on the other hand, employ flexible tines or spikes to loosen soil and remove weeds, suited for softer and more loosely packed soils. Both types serve essential roles in soil preparation, promoting better crop growth by enhancing soil aeration and structure.</p></p>
