<p><p>High Density EMG (HD-EMG) electrodes are advanced devices used for detecting and analyzing electrical activity in muscles with high spatial resolution. This technology significantly enhances the ability to diagnose neuromuscular disorders, monitor rehabilitation progress, and facilitate research in biomechanics.</p><p>The HD-EMG electrodes market is experiencing robust growth, fueled by the increasing prevalence of neuromuscular diseases, a rising aging population, and advancements in technology that improve electrode design and functionality.
