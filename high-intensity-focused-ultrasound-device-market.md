<p><p>The High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) device market is divided into two main types: diagnostic and therapeutic devices. Diagnostic HIFU devices are employed for imaging and assessing tissue characteristics, enabling precise localization of abnormalities. In contrast, therapeutic HIFU devices are designed for treatment purposes, delivering focused ultrasound energy to target and ablate tissues, commonly used in oncology for tumor destruction. Both types utilize ultrasound technology but serve different clinical applications in healthcare settings.</p></p>
