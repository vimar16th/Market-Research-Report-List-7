<p><p>The HJT (Heterojunction Technology) battery market is categorized into two main types: N-type and P-type. N-type batteries utilize a layer of n-type silicon that enhances efficiency and reduces recombination losses, making them ideal for high-performance applications. In contrast, P-type batteries employ a p-type silicon layer, traditionally more common but often less efficient. The distinction between these types influences their performance, production costs, and suitability for various applications, driving innovation and competition in the solar technology landscape.</p></p>
