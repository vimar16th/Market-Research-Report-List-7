<p><p>Home coffee and espresso machines are categorized into three main types: manually operated, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require users to control every aspect of brewing, offering a hands-on experience. Semi-automatic machines automate some processes, like water flow, while still allowing for user input on grind size and extraction time, striking a balance between control and convenience. Fully automatic machines do everything with minimal user intervention, providing convenience for those seeking quick, consistent results without extensive involvement.</p></p>
