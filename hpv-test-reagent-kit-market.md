In contrast, Molecular Hybridization involves hybridizing HPV DNA with labeled probes, allowing for the detection of specific viral types through signal generation. Both methods play a crucial role in cervical cancer screening and prevention, contributing to improved healthcare outcomes worldwide.</p></p>
