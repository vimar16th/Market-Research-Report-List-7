<p><p>The IC packing tray market encompasses various materials, each suited for specific applications. MPPE (Modified Polyphenylene Ether) offers excellent temperature resistance, making it ideal for high-performance packaging. PES (Polyethersulfone) is known for its durability and thermal stability, suitable for delicate electronic components. PS (Polystyrene) is lightweight and cost-effective, commonly used for standard packing needs. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) combines strength and impact resistance, making it popular for protective trays. Other materials include specialized composites and biodegradable options for niche applications.</p></p>
