This design is beneficial for achieving thorough rinsing without mechanical grip. In contrast, Side Grip Type rinser utilizes side mechanisms to grip the bottles, ensuring stability during the rinsing process. Both types are vital in industries like beverages and pharmaceuticals, enhancing product cleanliness and safety.</p></p>
