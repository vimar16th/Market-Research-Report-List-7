<p><p>The Laparoscopic Surgery Retrieval Pouch market is categorized into two main types: Manual Opening and Automatic Opening. Manual Opening pouches require the surgeon to manually open the pouch to retrieve specimens, offering control but potentially increasing operational time. In contrast, Automatic Opening pouches use mechanisms that allow for hands-free operation, facilitating quicker and more efficient specimen retrieval. The choice between these types depends on factors such as surgical preference, procedure complexity, and the need for speed and efficiency in surgeries.</p></p>
