<p><p>A Liquid Organic Hydrogen Carrier (LOHC) is a chemical compound that can store and transport hydrogen in a liquid form at ambient conditions. LOHCs are organic molecules that can reversibly absorb and release hydrogen through hydrogenation and dehydrogenation processes. This technology offers a safe and efficient way to store and transport hydrogen, addressing challenges related to hydrogen's low density and high flammability. LOHCs are gaining traction as a promising solution for hydrogen storage, particularly in applications such as energy storage, transportation, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Liquid Organic Hydrogen Carrier Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.3% during the forecast period. As the world transitions to a low-carbon economy, LOHCs are poised to play a critical role in enabling the widespread adoption of hydrogen as a clean energy carrier.</p></p>
