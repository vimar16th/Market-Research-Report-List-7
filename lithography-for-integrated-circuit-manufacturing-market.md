<p><p>Lithography is a crucial process in integrated circuit manufacturing, enabling the transfer of circuit patterns onto semiconductor wafers. The market comprises primarily two types: EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography and DUV (Deep Ultraviolet) lithography. EUV lithography utilizes shorter wavelengths to achieve finer resolutions, essential for producing advanced nodes in chips, while DUV lithography, employing longer wavelengths, remains prevalent for established node technologies. Each type serves different manufacturing requirements, influencing design complexity and production efficiency in semiconductor fabrication.</p></p>
