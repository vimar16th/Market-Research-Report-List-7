<p><p>The luminaire market comprises various types, each serving distinct lighting needs. LED luminaires offer energy efficiency and long lifespan, making them popular for modern applications. LFL luminaires, or fluorescent lighting, are used in commercial settings for their wide distribution of light. HID luminaires are favored for outdoor and industrial use due to their high intensity. Halogen and incandescent luminaires, while providing warm light, are becoming less common due to lower efficiency.</p></p>
