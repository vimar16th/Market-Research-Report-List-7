<p><strong>The Magnetic Sheet Fanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Magnetic Sheet Fanners market is categorized into two main types: permanent magnet and electromagnetic. Permanent magnet fanners use fixed magnets to create a steady magnetic field, providing consistent performance without the need for electricity, making them energy-efficient and reliable. In contrast, electromagnetic fanners utilize electric current to generate a variable magnetic field, allowing for adjustable force and control. This flexibility can be advantageous in applications requiring precise handling of metallic sheets in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
