Bonnell coils are hourglass-shaped and interconnected, offering a traditional design with good stability. Offset coils have a unique shape that enhances adaptability and motion isolation. Marshall coils, also known as pocket coils, are individually wrapped to provide targeted support and minimize partner disturbance, catering to diverse sleep preferences.</p></p>
