<p><p>The Medium Intensity Aircraft Warning Lights market encompasses three main types: low-intensity, medium intensity, and high-intensity warning lights. Low-intensity lights are used for structures under 150 feet, providing minimal visibility. Medium intensity lights, designed for structures between 150 to 500 feet, offer enhanced visibility while reducing glare. High-intensity lights are intended for tall structures over 500 feet, ensuring maximum visibility over long distances. Each type serves a specific height requirement to enhance aviation safety and prevent collisions with obstacles.</p></p>
