<p><p>Monochrome LCD display modules are categorized into several types based on their technology. TN (Twisted Nematic) displays offer fast response times and lower costs but have limited viewing angles. STN (Super Twisted Nematic) improves contrast and viewing angles, making it suitable for various applications. VA (Vertical Alignment) displays provide superior color reproduction and broader viewing angles. TFT (Thin Film Transistor) technology enables vibrant colors and high resolution, ideal for advanced applications. Other types may include technologies with niche applications or emerging innovations.</p></p>
