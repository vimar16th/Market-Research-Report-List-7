Linear Multi-head Weighing Machines utilize a straight-line arrangement of weighing heads, ideal for high-speed operations and bulk packaging applications. Conversely, Rotary Multi-head Weighing Machines feature a circular setup, providing efficient product handling and weighing with less space required.
