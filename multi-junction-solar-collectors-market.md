<p><p>The multi-junction solar collectors market is divided into two main types: concentrating and non-concentrating collectors. Concentrating type collectors utilize mirrors or lenses to focus sunlight onto a small area, increasing efficiency and energy output, often suited for large-scale applications. Non-concentrating type collectors directly capture sunlight without focusing, making them simpler and more affordable, typically used in residential and commercial installations. Both types cater to varying needs based on efficiency, cost, and application scale in the renewable energy landscape.</p></p>
