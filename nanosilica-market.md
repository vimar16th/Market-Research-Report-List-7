<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NanoSilica market is primarily categorized into two types: P-type and S-type. P-type nanoSilica is generally produced using a precipitation method, resulting in a higher purity and specific morphology, ideal for applications in rubber and coatings. In contrast, S-type nanoSilica, manufactured through a sol-gel process, offers unique properties like enhanced surface area, making it suitable for advanced applications such as electronics and pharmaceuticals. Each type serves distinct industry needs, influencing their market demand and growth trajectory.</p></p>
