<p><p>The nerve repair biomaterial market is categorized into several types, including nerve conduits, nerve grafts, nerve wraps, and others. Nerve conduits are tubular structures that guide regenerating nerve fibers across gaps. Nerve grafts involve transplanting tissue to bridge the nerve injury. Nerve wraps are used to encase damaged nerves, promoting healing and protection. Other biomaterials may include injectable hydrogels or scaffolds that provide support for nerve regeneration. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing nerve repair and recovery.</p></p>
