Antimicrobial coatings inhibit the growth of microorganisms, promoting hygiene in medical and consumer applications. Protective coatings shield surfaces from damage due to environmental factors. Optical coatings enhance light transmission and reduce glare, making them ideal for lenses and display technologies. Each type serves unique, valuable functions.</p></p>
