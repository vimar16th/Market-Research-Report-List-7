<p><strong>The Organic Photodetector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Based Organic Photodetector</li><li>Thermal Organic Photodetector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic photodetector market is primarily divided into two types: photon-based organic photodetectors and thermal organic photodetectors. Photon-based organic photodetectors utilize organic semiconductor materials to detect light by generating charge carriers in response to photon absorption, making them suitable for applications in imaging and communication. In contrast, thermal organic photodetectors operate based on temperature changes induced by absorbed light, converting thermal energy into an electrical signal. This type is often used in environmental monitoring and industrial applications.</p></p>
