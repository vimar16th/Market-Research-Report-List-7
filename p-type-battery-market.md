<p><p>The P-Type Battery market primarily includes technologies like PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) and BSF (Back Surface Field) batteries. PERC batteries enhance efficiency by utilizing a passivation layer, improving light capture and reducing recombination losses. BSF batteries utilize a rear surface field to reflect unabsorbed light back into the cell, further boosting efficiency. Both technologies aim to optimize energy production, reduce production costs, and enhance overall solar panel performance in the growing renewable energy market.</p></p>
