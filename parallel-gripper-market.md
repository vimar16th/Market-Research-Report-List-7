<p><p>The parallel gripper market encompasses two main types: pneumatic and electric parallel grippers. Pneumatic parallel grippers utilize compressed air for actuation, offering rapid movement and high gripping force, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Electric parallel grippers, on the other hand, use electric motors for actuation, providing precise control and programmability, ideal for tasks requiring delicate handling and variable force. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and manufacturing for their efficiency and adaptability in various industrial processes.</p></p>
