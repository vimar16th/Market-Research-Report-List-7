<p><p>The pediatric support corset market encompasses two primary types: underbust and overbust corsets. Underbust corsets provide support below the bust, promoting spinal alignment and stability while allowing freedom of movement for the upper body. Conversely, overbust corsets extend above the bust, offering increased support for the spine and ribcage, making them suitable for conditions requiring more comprehensive torso support. Both types aim to aid in developmental issues, improve posture, and enhance comfort in children with specific medical needs.</p></p>
