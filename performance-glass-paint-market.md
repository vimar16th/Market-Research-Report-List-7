<p><strong>The Performance Glass Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pyrolytic Coating</li><li>Sputtered Coating</li><li>Screen Coating</li><li>Spray Coating</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Performance glass paint encompasses various coating types used to enhance glass surfaces. Pyrolytic coating involves a heat-treated process for durability and resistance. Sputtered coating uses vapor deposition for a thin, uniform layer with high precision. Screen coating applies paint through a mesh screen for detailed designs, while spray coating offers versatility for quick applications and intricate finishes. Other methods include electrostatic painting and dip coating, catering to specialized needs in the market, enhancing aesthetics and functionality of glass.</p></p>
