<p><p>The PET nuclear imaging devices market comprises three main types: standalone PET, PET/CT, and PET/MRI. Standalone PET systems focus solely on positron emission tomography imaging, providing detailed metabolic information. PET/CT combines PET with computed tomography, enhancing anatomical localization along with functional imaging. PET/MRI merges PET's metabolic insights with magnetic resonance imaging's superior soft tissue contrast, offering comprehensive diagnostic capabilities. Each type addresses varying clinical needs, facilitating precise disease detection and monitoring in oncology and other medical fields.</p></p>
