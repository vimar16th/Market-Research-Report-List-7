<p><p>The pH sensor cartridge market comprises primarily two types: single junction and double junction pH sensor cartridges. Single junction cartridges contain one electrode and are typically simpler and more cost-effective, suitable for less demanding applications. In contrast, double junction cartridges feature two separate chambers for reference and measuring solutions, enhancing stability and longevity in challenging environments. This design prevents contamination, making them ideal for more sensitive or complex applications, thus catering to diverse industrial needs in an expanding market.</p></p>
