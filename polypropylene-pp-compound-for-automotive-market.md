<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) compounds for the automotive market encompass various types tailored for specific applications. Mineral-filled PP enhances rigidity and reduces costs, while Thermoplastic Polyolefins (TPO) combine flexibility and durability, making them ideal for exterior components. Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV) offer rubber-like properties for enhanced impact resistance. Glass-reinforced PP improves strength and thermal stability, crucial for structural parts. Additive concentrates optimize performance characteristics, such as UV resistance or color. Other market segments include custom blends for unique automotive needs, ensuring versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
