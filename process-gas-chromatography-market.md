<p><ul><li>Gas-solid chromatography (GSC)</li><li>Gas-liquid chromatography (GLC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Process Gas Chromatography market is categorized into Gas-Solid Chromatography (GSC) and Gas-Liquid Chromatography (GLC). GSC involves the separation of gases as they pass through a solid stationary phase, ideal for analyzing gaseous samples and detecting trace impurities. In contrast, GLC uses a liquid stationary phase to separate volatile components in a gas mixture, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, including environmental monitoring and petrochemical analysis. Both methods are essential for precise gas analysis.</p></p>
