<p><p>R-C oscillators are essential circuits that generate sinusoidal waveforms, primarily used in signal generation and clock applications. The Wien Bridge Oscillator utilizes a bridge circuit with resistors and capacitors to produce stable sine waves. The Twin-T Oscillator employs a T-shaped network of resistors and capacitors to achieve frequency stability. The Phase-Shift Oscillator generates sine waves through multiple RC phase-shifting networks, allowing for frequency adjustment. Each of these oscillator types has unique characteristics, making them suitable for various electronic applications.</p></p>
