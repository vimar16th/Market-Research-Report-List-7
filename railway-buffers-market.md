<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Buffers</li><li>Friction Buffers</li><li>Rubber Buffers</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Railway buffers are essential components designed to absorb the kinetic energy of trains during deceleration or collisions, ensuring safety. The hydraulic buffers use fluid dynamics for effective energy absorption, while friction buffers employ mechanical friction to dampen impacts. Rubber buffers utilize elastic properties to minimize shocks. Other types may include spring or composite buffers which combine various materials for enhanced performance. Each type provides distinct advantages in terms of efficiency, maintenance, and operational suitability in the railway industry.</p></p>
