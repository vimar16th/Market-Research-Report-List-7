<p><p>The resorbable polymers market includes various types, each with unique properties and applications. Polylactic Acid (PLA) is widely used in medical devices and packaging due to its biodegradability. Polyglycolic Acid (PGA) offers rapid resorption, making it ideal for sutures. Polycaprolactone is valued for its flexibility and slower degradation rate. Polysaccharides serve as natural polymers in drug delivery. Bio-polyesters combine biodegradability with functional features, while agro-polymers utilize renewable resources. Proteins provide biocompatibility for medical applications, enhancing tissue regeneration technologies.</p></p>
