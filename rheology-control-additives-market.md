<p><p>Rheology control additives are substances used to modify the flow and deformation behavior of materials. **Powder rheology control additives** are fine particulate materials that enhance viscosity, stability, and texture in dry or semi-solid formulations, commonly used in construction, coatings, and pharmaceuticals. **Liquid rheology control additives** are fluid-based solutions that improve flow properties, prevent sagging, and ensure uniformity in liquid systems like paints, adhesives, and personal care products. Both types optimize performance by tailoring rheological properties to specific application needs.</p></p>
