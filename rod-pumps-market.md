<p><p>Rod pumps, or sucker rod pumps, are essential components in the oil and gas industry, primarily used for lifting crude oil from wells. They consist of a series of rods that connect a downhole pump to a surface motor, effectively extracting fluids from depths where natural pressure is insufficient. The efficiency and reliability of rod pumps have made them a preferred choice for many operators in various geological settings.</p><p>The Rod Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period, driven by increasing exploration and production activities worldwide.
