<p><p>The roller compactor market includes various types designed for efficient road construction and maintenance. The single drum vibratory roller features one large drum and is ideal for compaction of granular materials. The double drum vibratory roller has two drums, providing uniform compaction, making it suitable for asphalt applications. The tire roller utilizes rubber tires to achieve final compaction on surfaces, offering flexibility and minimal surface damage. Each type caters to specific compaction needs, enhancing overall project efficiency.</p></p>
