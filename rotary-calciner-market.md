<p><strong>The Rotary Calciner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary Rotary Calciner</li><li>Indirect Rotary Calciner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary calciner market consists of two main types: ordinary rotary calciners and indirect rotary calciners. Ordinary rotary calciners operate by directly exposing materials to a heat source, resulting in efficient heat transfer for various industrial processes. In contrast, indirect rotary calciners utilize an enclosed heating mechanism, minimizing contact between the material and combustion gases. This distinction allows for greater temperature control and reduced emissions, catering to applications requiring stringent environmental standards and product purity, thus expanding their market appeal.</p></p>
