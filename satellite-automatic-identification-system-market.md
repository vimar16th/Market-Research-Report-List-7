<p><p>The Satellite Automatic Identification System (AIS) market encompasses two primary transponder types: Class A and Class B. Class A transponders are designed for vessels over 300 gross tons and equipped to transmit detailed information, such as position, identification, and vessel status, compliant with international maritime regulations. Class B transponders are suitable for smaller vessels and provide essential tracking data with less detail. Both types enhance maritime safety, traffic management, and situational awareness in marine environments, catering to varying vessel sizes and operational needs.</p></p>
