<p><p>Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPMs) are advanced tools used for imaging and manipulating materials at the nanometer scale. The Atomic Force Microscope (AFM) employs a cantilever with a sharp tip to detect forces between the tip and the sample surface, providing high-resolution images. Laser Force Microscopes utilize laser beams to measure forces at the nanoscale, enhancing sensitivity. Magnetic Force Microscopes focus on magnetic properties by detecting interactions between the tip and the sample's magnetic fields, making them vital in materials science and nanotechnology.</p></p>
