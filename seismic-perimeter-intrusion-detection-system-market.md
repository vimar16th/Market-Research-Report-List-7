<p><p>The Seismic Perimeter Intrusion Detection System (PID) market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Seismic Sensors**: These detect ground vibrations caused by intruders, providing real-time alerts and enabling quick responses to potential breaches. </p><p>2. **Seismic Servers**: These analyze data from sensors, processing the signals to differentiate between genuine threats and false alarms, ensuring accurate monitoring.</p><p>3. **Seismic Receivers**: These receive and transmit the processed information from sensors to control systems, facilitating centralized monitoring and control over secured perimeters.</p></p>
