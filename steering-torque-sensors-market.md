<p><strong>The Steering Torque Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steering torque sensors market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. **Contact type sensors** utilize physical mechanisms to measure torque by detecting the force exerted on the steering system through mechanical components. Conversely, **non-contact type sensors** employ magnetic or optical methods to gauge torque without any physical contact, offering enhanced durability and reduced wear. Both types are crucial for advanced driver-assistance systems, contributing to improved vehicle safety and performance.</p></p>
