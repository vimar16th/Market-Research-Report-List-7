<p><p>The tapping fluid market is primarily divided into two types: coolant and lubricant. Coolants are designed to reduce heat generation during the drilling or machining process, enhancing tool life and preventing material deformation. They help in dissipating heat and improving surface finish. Conversely, lubricants minimize friction between the tool and workpiece, facilitating smoother operation and reducing wear. Both types are essential for optimizing performance, ensuring efficiency in metalworking processes, and achieving high-quality results while extending tool longevity.</p></p>
