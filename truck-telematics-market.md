<p><strong>The Truck Telematics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plug and Play Telematics</li><li>Hardwired Install Telematics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck telematics market comprises two primary types: Plug and Play telematics and Hardwired Install telematics. Plug and Play telematics systems offer easy installation, typically using a simple device that connects to the vehicle's OBD-II port, allowing for quick data collection without professional help. In contrast, Hardwired Install telematics systems require direct integration into the truck's electrical system, providing more comprehensive data tracking and enhanced functionality but necessitating professional installation for optimal performance. Both cater to varying user needs and preferences.</p></p>
