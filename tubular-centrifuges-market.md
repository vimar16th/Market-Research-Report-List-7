<p><p>Tubular centrifuges are specialized machines used for the separation of solid-liquid mixtures based on differences in density. They operate by spinning the mixture at high speeds, causing denser materials to move outward, while lighter materials are displaced inward. This technology is commonly utilized in industries such as chemical processing, food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment.</p><p>The Tubular Centrifuges Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for efficient separation processes across various sectors.
