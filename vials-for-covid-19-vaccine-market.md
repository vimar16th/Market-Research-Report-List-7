<p><p>The COVID-19 vaccine vial market is primarily segmented into two types: middle-borate borosilicate glass and low-borate borosilicate glass. Middle-borate borosilicate glass offers enhanced chemical durability and thermal resistance, making it suitable for storing vaccines that require stability. In contrast, low-borate borosilicate glass, with lower boron content, is often preferred for its lower thermal expansion, ensuring less risk of breakage and contamination. Both types are critical for maintaining the efficacy and safety of vaccines during storage and transport.</p></p>
